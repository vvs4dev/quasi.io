Quasi Designs is a strong platform to transform the creative home design ideas of homeowners into augmented reality. Our tool empowers users to visualize the best possible design and color solutions according to their individual styles and preferences.

During the furniture or color selection, homeowners invest a lot of energy and time to imagine how the chosen color scheme or furniture will loom in their home until it's ready.

However, our creative tool has fanatically simplified the selection process by transforming user's design anticipations into augmented reality. Hence, individuals can now easily envision furniture redesigning or new color scheme of their home within no time.

How the Platform Works

You may easily understand the function of our Quasi Design platform based on an example. Suppose you have to place a sofa/couch and a tea/coffee table in your living room. For this-

  • On the other side, if your prospect does not feel satisfied, he may follow the same procedure number of times until and unless he succeeds to get the preferable furniture option from the available menu
