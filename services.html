Color Selection Tool

Our color design platform allows homeowners to visualize their entire home. Our contemporary platform will provide adequate benefit whether it is a specific color scheme or a customized home design.

The platform allows homeowners to select from a menu of all the elements that can be manipulated on a house for instance, roof, gutter pipes, bricks, stone bench top, cabinets etc . Thus, rather than imagining, the tool offers liberty to visualize the chosen color scheme. Once the individual is satisfied with the selected combination, they will finalize the design. The complete process requires only a small span of time and little efforts.


Furniture Selection Tool

This impressive tool allows you to choose re-size and position a furniture piece, take a picture of the room you want it to go in and visualise how it looks in that space, that too with only a few clicks.

You may easily understand the function of our Quasi Designs platform based on an example. Suppose you have to place a sofa/couch and a tea/coffee table in your living room. For this-

  • Select the furniture, for instance, coffee table or sofa you want to try out
  • Select Augmented Reality and pick the image from your gallery or take a picture of your room
  • View your chosen product by re-sizing and positioning the product in your space.
