Our Vision

To provide a platform to homebuilders and furniture outlets, which work as a virtual assistant to help their clients envision and customize innovative home design ideas.

This invaluable tool will give homeowners a confidence, gratification and visibility on how the preferred design will emerge on their house.

The biggest challenge for homeowners at the time of selecting their color scheme or furniture is to imagine how the chosen, for instance, roof tiles, bricks, sofa, stone bench top or coffee table would appear on their home. Moreover, many people face difficulty to choose and coordinate colors to enhance and retain the beauty of their indoor space.

Home décor service providers often face difficulty too in representing the ideal solution to clients even though they have countless fresh ideas in their minds. Thus, our platform gives home décor companies and furniture groups an opportunity to take homeowners in the world of augmented reality where they can transform ideas into true work of art.
